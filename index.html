What is EcoFlare?

EcoFlare transforms dog waste into biogas to power street lamps, providing sustainable and eco-friendly lighting.

Why It Matters

By reducing waste and using renewable energy, EcoFlare helps fight climate change and keeps communities clean.

How It Works

People deposit dog waste in special collection units. A biodigester processes it into biogas, which powers street lamps, reducing fossil fuel consumption.
